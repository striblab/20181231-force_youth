Police are using force against black youth less often
Police use-of-force incidents where the suspect was a black male aged 16 to 24 has dropped about eight percentage points since 2008.
Source: Minneapolis Police Department | Graphic by Jeff Hargarten, Star Tribune
Police still more often use force against black youth
Black youth alone comprised about a quarter of all police use-of-force incidents from 2008 through 2018.
Source: Minneapolis Police Department | Graphic by Jeff Hargarten, Star Tribune
